Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Folio?

Download Folio from the App Store and open it. The app will guide you through the initial setup. No account or sign-in is required.

Does Folio work offline?

Yes. All your data is stored locally on your device. Folio works fully without an internet connection.

Is my data backed up?

Folio supports iCloud backup as part of your standard iOS device backup. Your data is included automatically if you have iCloud Backup enabled in Settings.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual entries within the app, or remove all data by deleting the app from your device. No data is stored on external servers.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Please use the contact button below to send us a description of the issue, your device model, and iOS version. We aim to respond within 2 business days.
