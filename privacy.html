Privacy Policy

Folio  ·  Effective Date: May 18, 2026  ·  Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Folio does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Your data never leaves your device.

1. Information We Collect

Folio does not collect any personal information, usage data, analytics, or identifiers of any kind. No data is sent to any server operated by us or any third party.

2. Data Storage

All data you create within Folio is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage APIs. We have no access to this data at any time.

3. iCloud

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your Folio data may be included in your personal iCloud backup as part of the standard iOS backup process. This backup is managed entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

4. Third-Party Services

Folio does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or tracking services.

5. Children's Privacy

Folio does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for use by all ages.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last Updated" date above. Any changes will be reflected on this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
